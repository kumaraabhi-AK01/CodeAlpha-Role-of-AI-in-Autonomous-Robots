# CodeAlpha Robotics & Automation Internship

## Task 4: Role of AI in Autonomous Robots

### Project Overview
This mini project explores the role of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in autonomous robots. It explains how AI technologies such as machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and sensor fusion enable robots to perceive their environment, make intelligent decisions, and perform tasks with minimal human intervention.

### Objectives
- Study the role of AI in autonomous robotics.
- Understand key AI technologies used in robotic systems.
- Explore real-world applications of AI-powered robots.
- Analyze the advantages, challenges, and future trends of AI in automation.
